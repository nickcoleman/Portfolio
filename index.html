About

I’m a full stack web developer, living in Park City, Utah. I first started coding as a Assembler Language programmer on Missile-Space Track systems in the US Air Force. I spent my AF career in the IT field managing computer-communications infrastructure and software projects. And, I retired as an Air Force Lieutenant Colonel.

Post military I worked with a number of start-up software companies … eventually starting my own that focused on Customer Relationship Management software.

I’ve always loved the creative process and teamwork that goes into developing a software product. So, I refocused my second career into that passion as a software developer with a focus on Full Stack Web Development

I also enjoy helping others and volunteered as a mentor with PandoLabs to train low-income service industry and rural workers to become full-stack web developers. And, I also help self-motivated individuals to learn to code through through FreeCodeCamp Salt Lake City.
